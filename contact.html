Our Information

Contact DiscPro Printing & Graphics today, and let us get started on your next beautiful print job.

DiscPro opened its doors in 1999, in a 12,000 square foot building. We started small and grew at a steady, manageable pace, evolving with the Houston market, and adapting as technology advanced.

We've succeeded because our owners know the nuts and bolts of the business; all three began their careers in print production and have years of experience in sales, management and customer service. We've been around since the days of X-acto knives and film. And today we have one of the most advanced production facilities in Houston.

Today, we occupy over 21,000 square feet, and we are the only printer in Houston with a brand new Heidelberg CX102 6 color, 40-inch press, said to be best commercial press on the market. Our story unfolds, and DiscPro grows, every day. Contact us today to be part of it.
